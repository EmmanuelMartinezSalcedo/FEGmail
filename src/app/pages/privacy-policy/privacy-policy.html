2. GENERAL INFORMATION

2.1 Who is the Controller of your personal data?

The data controller is Flow Box, an email management and organization application hosted on cloud services (Azure, Render, and Vercel).

Contact: flowbox29@gmail.com

2.2 What do we process your personal data for?

We process your personal data in order to:

  • Provide secure access to your Gmail account via OAuth authentication.
  • Organize and classify emails automatically.
  • Improve user experience and maintain application security.
  • Respond to support requests.

2.3 What is the legal basis that legitimizes the processing of your personal data?

The legal basis is your explicit consent, granted when you authorize Flow Box to access your Gmail account through OAuth.

2.4 How long do we keep your personal data?

We will retain your personal data only while your Flow Box account remains active. If you revoke access from Google or request deletion of your account, data will be erased within 30 days.

2.5 Who is responsible for keeping the data updated?

The user is responsible for ensuring that the personal data provided is accurate and up to date.

2.6 Who may be recipients of your personal data?

  • Google: only for authentication services (OAuth) and authorized Gmail access.
  • Hosting and cloud service providers: required for application operation.
  • We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties for commercial purposes.

2.7 Personal data security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Data encryption (HTTPS, TLS).
  • Use of JWT tokens for session management.
  • Access restrictions and internal auditing.

2.8 What are your data protection rights and how can you exercise them?

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw the consent you previously granted.

To exercise these rights, you can contact us at: flowbox29@gmail.com
