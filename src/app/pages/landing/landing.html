Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI labeling work?

Our AI analyzes the content, sender, and metadata in your emails to automatically suggest and apply relevant labels.

Is my email data secure?

Yes! We only process your emails to apply labels and never store any email content. We use OAuth2 for secure Gmail access and follow strict privacy guidelines.

Can I use my existing Gmail labels?

Absolutely! We seamlessly integrate with your existing Gmail labels and you can choose to enhance them with our AI-powered suggestions.

What if I want to cancel?

You can cancel anytime. Your Gmail labels will remain intact, and we'll immediately stop processing your new emails.
